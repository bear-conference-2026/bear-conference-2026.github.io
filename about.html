Our Mission

The 15th Annual BEAR PGR Conference is a postgraduate-led event exploring cutting-edge research enabled by the Birmingham Environment for Academic Research. Our theme for 2026, AI x Integrity, focuses on the intersection of advanced computing and ethical research practices.

Why Attend?

This conference provides a unique platform for researchers to present their results, learn about innovative techniques across various fields, and network with peers who use BlueBEAR and HPC approaches.
