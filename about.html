About the Conference

The 15th Annual BEAR PGR Conference: Bridging Research, Innovation, and Computing.

Our Mission

The BEAR (Birmingham Environment for Academic Research) Conference is a premier event for postgraduate researchers to showcase work enabled by High Performance Computing. Originally created to give PhD students experience in leading academic events, it has grown into a vital interdisciplinary hub.

This year, under the theme "AI x Integrity," we explore the ethical boundaries and technical possibilities of artificial intelligence in modern research.

Why Attend?

  • Raise Your Profile: Present your research results in a professional yet welcoming environment.
  • Interdisciplinary Networking: Connect with peers and experts from across the University who use BlueBEAR and HPC approaches.
  • Skill Development: Learn about innovative techniques being used across diverse fields—from Biosciences to Economics.
  • Prizes & Recognition: Compete for "Best Talk" and "Best Poster" awards, generously sponsored by Lenovo.

The 2026 Theme: AI x Integrity

As AI tools become integrated into every stage of the research lifecycle, the 15th Annual Conference focuses on the dual pillars of Performance and Ethics. We aim to answer: How can we harness the power of BEAR's computing clusters to drive AI innovation while maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity?

Join us to discuss cross-cutting topics such as next-generation technology, algorithmic fairness, and the future of knowledge dissemination in a world shaped by Large Language Models and automated data analysis.

Organised by PGRs, for PGRs

The conference is entirely postgraduate-led, providing the committee with invaluable experience in project management, communication, and leadership. This grassroots approach ensures the event remains focused on the needs and interests of the junior research community at the University of Birmingham.

Our Partners

We are proud to be supported by Lenovo and the Advanced Research Computing (ARC) team. Their support allows us to provide high-quality catering, professional proceedings, and exciting giveaway prizes for our attendees.
