Our Mission

The BEAR (Birmingham Environment for Academic Research) Conference is a premier event for postgraduate researchers to showcase work enabled by High Performance Computing. This grassroots approach ensures the event remains focused on the needs of the junior research community.

Why Attend?

Connect with peers from across the University, learn about cutting-edge AI methodologies, and compete for "Best Talk" and "Best Poster" awards.
