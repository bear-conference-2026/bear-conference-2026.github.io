Our Mission

The BEAR (Birmingham Environment for Academic Research) Conference is a premier event for postgraduate researchers to showcase work enabled by High Performance Computing. It has grown into a vital interdisciplinary hub for innovation.

Why Attend?

This conference provides a unique platform for researchers to present their results, learn about innovative techniques across various fields, and network with peers who use BlueBEAR and HPC approaches.
