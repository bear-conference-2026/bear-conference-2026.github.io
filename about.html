Our Mission

The BEAR (Birmingham Environment for Academic Research) Conference is a premier event for postgraduate researchers to showcase work enabled by High Performance Computing.

This year, under the theme "AI x Integrity," we explore the ethical boundaries and technical possibilities of artificial intelligence in modern research.

Why Attend?

This conference provides a unique platform for researchers to present their results, learn about innovative techniques across various fields, and network with peers who use BlueBEAR and HPC approaches.

Also, stand a chance to compete for "Best Talk" and "Best Poster" awards, generously sponsored by Lenovo.
